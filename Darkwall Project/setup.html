Getting Started - Free

goto the "Start Menu"

 
 

goto the "My Computer"

 
 

goto the "Network"

 
 

goto the "Network and Sharing Center"

 
 

goto the "Set up a new connection or network"

 
 

goto the "Connect to a workplace"

 
 

goto the "No, create a new connection"

 
 

goto the "Use my Internet Connection"

 
 

In "Internet Address" enter "darkwall.ddns.net"

 
 

In "User name" enter "guest"

 

In "Password" enter "darkwall"

 
 

Wait for connection to establish

 
 

Setup Complete
